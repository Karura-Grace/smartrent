Frequently Asked Questions
Click a question to expand the answer
How do I pay my rent?

Go to the Payments section, choose your amount, and select M-Pesa, card, or bank transfer. You'll receive a confirmation SMS and email immediately.

What happens if I miss a payment?

A late fee may apply after the grace period (usually 5 days). You'll receive reminders before and after the due date. Contact your property manager early if you're having difficulty.

Can I download my payment receipts?

Yes. Go to Payments → History, find the transaction, and click the download icon. Receipts are PDF format.

How do I submit a maintenance request?

Open the Maintenance tab, click New Request, describe the issue and attach photos. Your manager will be notified and you can track the status in real time.

How do I update my contact details?

Go to Settings → Profile. Update your name, email or phone and click Save changes.

How do I change or reset my password?

Go to Settings → Security → Change password. You'll need your current password. If forgotten, use Forgot password on the login page.

Where can I view my lease agreement?

Your lease is available under My Lease in the sidebar. You can view, download, or print it anytime.
